Models

合作模式

crew@meje ~ diff models/partnership models/transfer models/commission
MODEL A

共同成长合作

标准 · 推荐

  • Start without upfront development cost
  • MEJE Works shares resources and risk
  • IP is registered as a joint asset of both parties
  • 100-day operation revenue distributed by agreed structure; ratio re-balanced from Season 2
  • Self-use revenue (concerts · SNS · in-house goods) belongs to the client
MODEL B

IP转让开发

IP资产化优先

  • Proceed with reasonable development cost
  • All rights to worldview, characters, and platform transferred to the client
  • Operation handled by MEJE Works; revenue shared by agreed ratio
  • Full control over the entire project
  • Free to use and expand after delivery
MODEL C

单项委托

部分委托 · 协议制

  • Partial commission: worldview only / character only / encyclopedia only
  • IP 100% belongs to the client
  • Operation not included (delivery-only)
  • Suitable when the client has its own operating team
  • Specifications and scope determined separately by agreement

Specific IP rights structure, operation revenue split, self-use scope, and Season 2 conversion terms are decided by agreement based on project nature. Hybrid models beyond the above are also possible.
